The Strokes

'The Strokes' is an alternative rock band. Here are some fun facts about them:

  • The Strokes formed in New York City in 1998.
  • They were massively influential in sparking the post-punk and garage rock revivals.
  • Arguably more popular bands like the Arctic Monkeys, The Killers, and Kings of Leon were influenced by the Strokes.
  • Lead singer and primary songwriter Julian Casablancas also leads a more experimental band called the Voidz.
